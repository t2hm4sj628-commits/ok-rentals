# OK RENTALS — Design Brief

## Aesthetic Direction
Dark luxury automotive with refined gold accents. Charcoal base (L:0.13) creates premium depth. Gold accents (L:0.75, C:0.15, H:45) applied sparingly to CTAs, hover states, and header underline. High-contrast typography hierarchy via Fraunces serif + GeneralSans sans-serif.

## Palette (OKLCH)
| Role | L | C | H | Usage |
|------|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| Background | 0.13 | 0 | — | Dark base |
| Card | 0.17 | 0 | — | Elevated sections |
| Foreground | 0.95 | 0 | — | Primary text |
| Primary/Accent | 0.75 | 0.15 | 45 | Gold buttons, hovers |
| Border | 0.22 | 0 | — | Card dividers |
| Muted | 0.25 | 0 | — | Secondary elements |

## Typography
| Tier | Font | Use |
|------|------|-----|
| Display | Fraunces | Hero, section heads, brand |
| Body | General Sans | Paragraphs, UI labels |
| Mono | Geist Mono | Pricing, details |

## Structural Zones
- **Header**: Dark card bg (0.17 L), gold underline border, "OK RENTALS" display serif
- **Hero**: Full-bleed charcoal, imagery focal point
- **Sections**: Alternating card (0.17 L) + background (0.13 L) rhythm
- **Footer**: Elevated card bg, CEO profile card, payment icons, booking info

## Components & Motion
- Card hover: +4px lift, luxury-lg shadow
- Buttons: Gold bg, smooth 0.4s ease transitions
- Text: High contrast white (0.95 L) on dark
- Animations: fade-in (0.5s), slide-up (0.4s) on scroll

## Differentiation
Premium automotive aesthetic via intentional depth, serif/sans pairing, and restrained gold accent. Every surface deliberate — no flat defaults. Micro-interactions reward interaction. Professional tone throughout.

## Constraints
- No rainbow palettes, no bounce animations
- Gold accent only on interactive elements and brand touches
- Minimum 7:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AAA)
- Smooth cubic-bezier transitions only

## Signature Detail
Elegant gold underline beneath "OK RENTALS" header — connects brand name to luxury aesthetic without overwhelming. CEO Instagram card in footer bridges human connection with luxury positioning.
